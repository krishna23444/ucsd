Planned Undergraduate Course Offerings 2005-2006

(Offerings subject to change. Revised May 9, 2006)
The following is a tentative course schedule for the 2005-06 academic year. The policy of the CSE department is to offer a fixed schedule each year. The department, however, cannot guarantee that this schedule will not change and promptly informs students of any changes. Students are encouraged to inform the department of any inconveniences proposed changes may cause. Contact Patricia Raczka at 858/534-3621 or raczka@cs.ucsd.edu for questions about this page.

Each entry of the following table gives the names of the instructors (Staff means that an instructor has not yet been assigned).

Repeat credit process for cross-referenced courses: Students may receive credit for CSE 20 or Math 15A, CSE 21 or Math 15B, CSE 100 or Math 176, CSE 101 or Math 188, and CSE 105 or Math 166. These courses have been cross-referenced, therefore, a petition is not required for using either course when completing major requirements. However, if you fail one of the courses listed above you will need to retake the SAME COURSE for REPEAT CREDIT. If you use another course that is equivalent, the repeat credit is not automatic and you will need to petition for repeat credit.

The Freshman Seminars are limited to 20 students. Each is offered for one unit of credit. First-year students will be given priority for enrollment. These courses are designated P / NP and may be taken pass / no pass only.
