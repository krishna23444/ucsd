Research Area

My research interests are in database systems and theory. Most recently, I've been focusing on querying semi-structured data, static analysis for XML-based mediators, XML documents with incomplete information, and automatic verification of interactive data-driven Web applications. I am also interested in the theory of query languages and logic.
